Don't Fret

Don't Fret is the project that has defined the last three years of my work, and I'm incredibly excited to finally share it as we get closer to release.

My contributions span the full character pipeline, including character modeling from concept to final in-game assets, design refinement for stronger translation into 3D, high poly sculpting, retopology, UV unwrapping, optimization, and complex rigging support. This project pushed me to think and operate more like a full game developer, balancing artistic vision with technical performance, so it felt right to feature it at the top of my portfolio.

  • Long-term production experience across character builds, cleanup passes, and sculpting polish.
  • A major recent project that shows where much of my time and energy has been focused.
  • A strong snapshot of the stylized horror work I want to keep building on in my portfolio.
