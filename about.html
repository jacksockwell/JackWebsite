More about me!

My name is Jack Sockwell. I'm 20 years old, originally from Pensacola, Florida, and I now live in Saint Clair, Pennsylvania. I'm a 3D artist, and believe it or not, I've been working in Blender for over 10 years. I actually started out as a Five Nights at Freddy's accuracy modeler, and if you know, you know.

A lot of my work sits between stylized and horror characters, and every once in a while I also make environments. I like being involved in more than one part of the pipeline, so I usually enjoy handling concept refining, modeling, and rigging instead of only focusing on one step.

I use Blender and Substance Painter the most. I love sculpting, making driver-based rigs, and absolutely NOT making UVs. I'm also really into Unreal Engine, especially seeing how characters and assets fit into a bigger final look once everything starts coming together. A lot of my work starts from concept art, or from scratch if I'm the one making the concept myself lol.

With Don't Fret in particular, the flow we had is: the concept artist makes sick ass concepts, I get sent them and drop them into the Blender project, I make the model and tweak the design in a couple ways, and then once the model is retopologized and optimized, my favorite part starts, sculpting the high poly.

More than anything, I want this site to show the work I've produced and poured my soul into. I'm always trying to push my work further, improve pipelines, work on my anatomy, and make each project stronger than the last. As of 4/7/2026, it's still pretty incomplete, but I like the idea of just having the website published and pushing out updates.
